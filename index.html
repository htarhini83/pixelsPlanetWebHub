Energy Prices Lebanon Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Energy Prices Lebanon, an Android application dedicated to delivering the latest fuel and energy prices within Lebanon. Leveraging modern Android features such as Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, our app offers a user-friendly interface and a host of functionalities. In addition to providing updated price information, Energy Prices Lebanon serves quick feed bites for easy reading, support for dark mode, an interactive chart to visualize price trends, and a tariff calculator to help you estimate your energy costs.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy outlines the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use Energy Prices Lebanon, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

If you have any concerns or questions regarding our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at tarhini.hassan@gmail.com. We are committed to ensuring a transparent and safe environment for all our users.
